Pure products

A story of products and passion

We serve artisanally grown quality products, prepared in our own kitchen.

Taste our unrivalled freshness

We have a passion for pure, fresh ingredients. Sustainably grown, delivered fresh every day and artisanally prepared in our own kitchen. With passion, terroir and love for the craft. That is how we make the difference.

Fresh, fast and above all delicious

We are open 7 days a week and Brasserie Rubens is always bustling. This gives us optimal rotation of ingredients. This efficient workflow is the result of nearly a century of experience.

Pure craftsmanship

Brasserie Rubens serves as many sustainable quality and short-chain products on your plate as possible. Pure, honest ingredients from sustainable growers and regional farmers.
