Hands-on student wanted!

Want to roll up your sleeves during the holidays and/or weekends? Become a student worker at Brasserie Rubens! Together with our enthusiastic team, you serve guests in the oldest and most atmospheric brasserie in Knokke.

What we expect from you

During service: serving drinks and dishes, clearing tables.

Before and after service: mise en place, sorting glassware, restocking fridges, …

Do you have plenty of drive and want to dive in? No worries if you have little experience - we will teach you the tricks of the trade.
